Natural Beauty

"Nature always wears the colors of the spirit." - Ralph Waldo Emerson

Nature refers to the world and everything in it that is not created by humans. It includes all living things, such as plants, animals, and humans, as well as the non-living elements of the earth, such as water, air, and minerals. The natural world can be observed and studied through the sciences of biology, ecology, geology, and astronomy, among others.

"Look deep into nature, and then you will understand everything better." - Albert Einstein

Natural phenomena, such as weather patterns, earthquakes, and volcanic eruptions, are governed by natural laws and processes. The study of these phenomena and their underlying processes is essential for understanding the functioning of the earth and its ecosystems.

"Study nature, love nature, stay close to nature. It will never fail you." - Frank Lloyd Wright
